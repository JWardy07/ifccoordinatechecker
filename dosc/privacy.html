IFC Coordinate Checker Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Overview

IFC Coordinate Checker is designed to process IFC model files locally on your device. The app helps check model positioning, origin, georeferencing, units, storeys and coordinate risk before coordination or federation.

Files you select

The app only accesses IFC files that you choose through the system file picker. Imported files are used so the app can analyse coordinate and georeferencing information and produce a local report.

Local processing

IFC Coordinate Checker processes selected IFC files locally on-device. The app does not require you to upload IFC models to a server in order to analyse them.

Reports and exports

CSV report export is user-initiated. Exported reports are saved only where you choose to save them.

Assistant features

Where available, the app may provide Apple Intelligence-style assistance to explain coordinate findings. The assistant is grounded in deterministic results already calculated by the checker. It does not decide PASS, WARNING or FAIL results, and it does not replace the deterministic checking engine.

Data collection

IFC Coordinate Checker does not collect personal information, does not require an account, and does not include analytics or advertising tracking.

Network access

The app is intended to work offline for IFC checking. It does not require cloud upload or an online service to analyse IFC files.
